How it works

How is a request counted?

Simple by design: a request matches a concrete engine action.

SearchEvery search typed by a visitor counts as one request (the engine responds from the 3rd keystroke by default, like most modern engines)
IndexingIndexing a batch of 1,000 products counts as 10 requests
API callsEvery call to the management or search API counts as one request
OverageBeyond the volume included in your plan, the extra usage is billed at the per-tier rate shown on your plan — never an abrupt service cutoff

Frequently asked questions

What people ask us most about the Search API.

How do I get started?

Create a key on the Free plan (no credit card required), index a first batch of products, run a search. The quickstart guide fits in 3 API calls.

Can I change plans or cancel anytime?

Yes. Monthly billing, no long-term commitment. Change plans or cancel whenever you want, no penalty.

Does the Heurix engine replace Algolia or Elasticsearch?

It's not a layer on top of existing solutions: it's a different engine, specialized in catalogs where references matter — dimensions, sizes, standards, vintages. The Search API makes sense as soon as you don't yet have a dedicated engine, or yours fails on this kind of search.

What happens if I exceed my request quota?

The service keeps working normally — the extra usage is simply billed at the per-tier rate shown on your plan. You're notified before any significant overage, no surprise cutoff.

Can I create rules for my own industry?

Three packs ship with the engine (hardware, fashion, industry) and cover most technical catalogs. On Scale plans, a custom pack can be designed for your specific references.
